# **Professional Development Action Plan**

This Action Plan outlines my key development objectives based on insights gained from my Skills Matrix, SWOT Analysis, and reflections throughout the module. It provides a structured overview of the skills I aim to improve, the actions I will take, the resources I require, and the timeframe for completion.

---

# Action Plan

| **Development Area** | **Current Status / Identified Need** | **Actions to be Taken** | **Resources Required** | **Success Criteria** | **Target Completion Date** |
|----------------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------------|--------------------------|------------------------|------------------------------|
| Advanced Statistics | Need deeper understanding of statistical modelling and interpretation to support dissertation-level analysis. | Enrol in advanced statistics coursework; complete weekly practice exercises; apply techniques in research project. | Online courses, textbooks, statistical software (Excel, Python). | Demonstrated ability to apply statistical methods accurately in coursework and dissertation preparation. | 3–4 months |
| Machine Learning | Require stronger foundations in ML algorithms and evaluation to support future data science work. | Complete ML modules; build small ML projects; seek feedback from tutors and peers. | Machine learning libraries, datasets, online tutorials. | Ability to build and evaluate ML models independently; improved performance in relevant assignments. | 4–6 months |
| Ethical Data Practices & Responsible AI | Need to strengthen understanding of ethical frameworks and responsible data use. | Study industry guidelines (e.g., ACM, IEEE); integrate ethical considerations into project work; reflect in e-portfolio. | Academic papers, ethics frameworks, module material. | Clear demonstration of ethical reasoning in assignments and project methodology. | Ongoing |
| Communication & Leadership | Improve confidence presenting to non-technical audiences and collaborating in teams. | Attend communication workshops; present findings in seminars; participate in group discussions; seek peer feedback. | Workshops, presentation tools, peer groups. | More effective delivery of presentations; improved peer feedback scores; increased leadership in team tasks. | 3–6 months |
| Research Methods Application | Need to enhance ability to plan and execute rigorous academic research. | Apply research methods in dissertation planning; conduct small pilot studies; consult supervisor for feedback. | Research journals, RM module resources. | Well-structured research proposal with clear methodology, ethical rationale, and feasibility. | 2–3 months |



## **Summary**

This Action Plan supports both my academic journey and professional growth. By strengthening technical expertise, research ethics, and communication abilities, I aim to develop into a well-rounded, responsible data science professional prepared for advanced research and industry practice.
