# SWOT Analysis

## Strengths
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL for data analysis and software development  
- Solid understanding of software engineering processes and design principles  
- Experience with penetration testing and secure development practices  
- Ability to work systematically through complex technical problems  
- Growing competence in research ethics and academic inquiry  

## Weaknesses
- Limited experience with advanced statistical modelling  
- Need deeper exposure to modern machine learning algorithms  
- Communication with non-technical stakeholders can be improved  
- Limited leadership experience in larger team environments  

## Opportunities
- Advanced courses in machine learning, AI, and applied statistics  
- Dissertation work offering space to apply research and analytical methods  
- Workshops and industry seminars on communication and leadership  
- Mentorship and peer-review opportunities in academic and professional settings  
- Rapid industry growth in AI ethics, governance, and responsible data practice  

## Threats
- Fast-paced evolution of tools and standards in data science  
- Increased competition in the AI/data sector for advanced roles  
- Risk of technical stagnation without deliberate upskilling  
- Ethical and regulatory challenges in data-driven environments
